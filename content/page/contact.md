## FAQs

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**

A: I recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for custom choreography. For standard performances, 2-3 weeks is typically sufficient.

**Q: Do you travel for performances?**

A: Yes! I'm available for performances locally, regionally, and beyond. Travel arrangements can be discussed during consultation.

**Q: Can you create custom choreography for my event?**

A: Absolutely. Custom choreography tailored to your event, music, and venue is part of my service. We'll collaborate to create something unique.

**Q: What if my venue has limited rigging capabilities?**

A: I work with venues to assess what's possible. I'm adaptable and can discuss alternative performance types based on your space and technical setup.

**Q: What is your cancellation policy?**

A: Please discuss terms during our initial consultation. Different packages and circumstances may have different policies.
